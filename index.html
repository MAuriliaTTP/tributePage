Tom Seaver

"The Franchise"

Here are some facts about Tom Seaver

  • Born November 17, 1944
  • Attended Fresno High School
  • Served the Marine Corps Reserve from 1962 to 1963
  • After serving he enrolled in Fresno City College
  • Originally drafted by the Atlanta Braves but the contract was voided since he wanted to pitch the college season but couldn't now that he had a major league contract
  • Signing rights were then awarded to the New York Mets in a lottery with two other teams
  • Made his major league debut with the Mets in 1967
  • Won the world series with the Mets in 1969 for the teams first ever title
  • Left the Mets through free agency in 1977 to join the Cincinnati Reds
  • His career included many awards such as the Cy Young and Rookie of the year
